River levels and flow - latest reading

  4. River levels and flow - latest reading

While most of the monitoring sites record data every 5 minutes, data is only retrieved from the monitoring sites every 30 minutes. Although the website is updated every 15 minutes, the data displayed on the website may not change as it has not yet been retrieved from the monitoring site.

Data is at NZ Standard Time. No allowance is made for daylight saving.

PLEASE NOTE: The information on this table is provisional. For more detailed information contact Waikato Regional Council. 

 

More information    

  • Rainfall and river level information - Waikato Regional Council
  • Lake level information: Waikato River hydro-electric scheme (including Lake Taupo) - Mercury Energy
  • Rainfall, lake and river level information: Tongariro Power Scheme - Genesis Energy
  • Current weather conditions, weather forecasts and severe weather bulletins - MetService.
  • Tidal forecasts - NIWA
  • Civil defence -  Waikato CDEM Group or the Ministry of Civil Defence and Emergency Management.
  • Geological hazards, such as earthquakes - Geonet
